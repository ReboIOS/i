Description
Minecraft Pocket Edition Lite was the free version of Minecraft MCPE. It didn't include most of the features that the full version of Minecraft has. Its latest version was up to date with Update 0.2. 1 of the full version, but without the world save functionality. The app was removed off of all app stores in 2014.
